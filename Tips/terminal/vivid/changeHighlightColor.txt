to change colors that appers in linux terminal
=================================================

1. terminal > edit > profile preferences > colors >

2. change background color to gray, if you like.

3. Palette, choose custom,
   then, lets see what colors are there in the terminal:
   cd Softwares; ls
   
   then, click on the Palette and replace the colors
   you can see instant change on the colors in the terminal.
