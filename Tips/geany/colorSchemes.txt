to add color schemes to geany
==============================

1. download schmes from github:
http://wiki.geany.org/themes/start

or,
https://github.com/codebrainz/geany-themes

then,
2. copy the themes from folder colorschmes to:
~/.config/geany/colorschemes/

3. restart geany,
view>change color schemes
my best is zenburn.
